Explore Planet Earth

🌍 Continents

Earth has 7 continents, each with unique cultures, climates, and landscapes.

🌊 Oceans

Covering 70% of the surface, oceans regulate climate and support life.

🌲 Forests

Forests are Earth's lungs, absorbing CO₂ and sheltering biodiversity.

🌡️ Climate

Earth's climate is changing rapidly due to human activity.

🏜️ Deserts

Dry, extreme environments covering 1/3rd of the land surface.

🌋 Mantle

The semi-solid layer beneath Earth's crust that drives plate movements.

🪓 Deforestation

Tree loss due to agriculture, urbanization, and logging is harming ecosystems.

🏔️ Himalayas

The highest mountain range in the world, still growing today.

🌌 Aurora

Stunning lights caused by solar wind interacting with Earth's magnetic field.
